How Our GAR System Works

1

Biological Degradation

Wastewater flows into the grease trap. Our Air Reactor begins biological degradation of organic compounds.

2

Air Reactor Process

Air reactor decomposes and removes foul odors immediately while breaking down organic substances and microbes.

3

Activated Sludge

Timer controller unit manages air injection cycles. Organics and microbes precipitate and decompose at the bottom.

4

Sedimentation

The air reactor degrades the organic matter in the processed water. The water is cleaned and drained to drainage pipe, reducing clogging risks and environmental impact.
